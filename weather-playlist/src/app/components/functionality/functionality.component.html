App functionality

What you can do with this app?

  • Create events for your daily life
  • View your schedule
  • Generate music based on the genres you picked
  • Search for musics

Music Generator

  • Search music with a word in the song or title
  • Use our media player at the bottom of page to play musics
  • Generate music based on the genres you pick

Create Events

  • Create events for your daily life
  • You can create event that is weekly or monthly
  • Add descriptions to your event

View Events

  • View the upcoming events you have
  • See details of events and make changes to events
  • The weather functionality will help user determine weather patterns for upcoming events
